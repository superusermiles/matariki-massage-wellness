# Matariki Massage & Wellness

## Project Overview
Matariki Massage & Wellness is a boutique massage and bodywork studio positioned as a calm, restorative retreat with a distinctly Aotearoa-inspired sense of grounding. The brand blends therapeutic skill with ritual-led relaxation: remedial massage, deep tissue release, pregnancy massage, hot stone therapy, and seasonal wellness packages. The studio should feel quiet, premium, nurturing, and deeply human rather than clinical or overly spiritual.

The website should present the business as trusted, refined, and restorative. It needs to convert first-time visitors into booking enquiries while also making returning clients feel they have found a sanctuary they want to revisit.

## Business Details
- **Business name:** Matariki Massage & Wellness
- **Industry:** Boutique massage therapy and wellness studio
- **Location:** Mount Maunganui, New Zealand
- **Service area:** Mount Maunganui, Tauranga, Papamoa
- **Primary offer:** Therapeutic massage and restorative body treatments in a private studio
- **Secondary offer:** Wellness packages, gift vouchers, and corporate recovery sessions by arrangement
- **Booking model:** By appointment only
- **Price positioning:** Premium but approachable

## Goals
1. Establish a calm, trustworthy premium brand presence.
2. Increase direct bookings and enquiry submissions.
3. Clearly communicate services, benefits, and pricing structure.
4. Differentiate the studio from generic day spas through a more personal, therapeutic positioning.
5. Support gift voucher purchases and wellness package enquiries.

## Target Audience
### Primary
- Women aged 28–55 seeking stress relief, muscular recovery, and intentional self-care.
- Professionals, mothers, and active locals who want expert treatment in a calm private setting.

### Secondary
- Couples purchasing gift vouchers.
- Visitors to Mount Maunganui wanting a restorative treatment while travelling.
- Clients seeking pregnancy massage or regular maintenance appointments.

## Brand Positioning
Matariki Massage & Wellness sits between clinical massage practice and indulgent spa experience. The studio offers expert hands-on treatment in a space that feels warm, considered, and emotionally restorative.

## Brand Voice
- Calm
- Grounded
- Reassuring
- Gentle but expert
- Thoughtful and understated

## Messaging Notes
- Avoid hard-sell or trendy wellness clichés.
- Speak with confidence and softness.
- Emphasise care, quality, privacy, and tailored treatment.
- Reference rhythm, rest, grounding, and renewal.

- Site feels distinctly premium, warm, and grounded.
